Biography

Hi, I'm Guobiao Li (李国标). I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher in the MAS Lab at Fudan University, working with Prof. Xinpeng Zhang. Before this, I majored in Computer Science and Technology (with a minor in Cyberspace Security) and obtained my Ph.D. degree from Fudan University.

I have a broad interest in most areas of information hiding and AI security, including steganography, watermarking, adversarial examples, and model backdoors.
